Linlin Cui

I am a coastal oceanographer. My research focuses on the development and application of numerical models to the coastal oceans with an interest in interdisciplinary research, including estuarine circulation, turbulence mixing, interactions between sediment process and biogeochemistry and aggregation from sediment and oil. Ocean models I use includes FVCOM, ROMS, and SCHISM.
